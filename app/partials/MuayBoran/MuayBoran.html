Muay Thai boran is a generic term for many of the muay thai systems that existed in Thailand before the art became regulated by the state in the early part of the 20th century. Muay Thai boran is the fore runner of modern muay thai. Through time, in certain areas of the country and because many other different factors, some styles of muay Thai boran became famous because of their effective techniques in contest.
